# Code of Conduct

We are committed to an open and welcoming project community. All participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Refrain from discriminatory or harassing behavior.
- Be open to constructive feedback.

Reporting
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers at: maintainers@yourdomain.example (replace with an appropriate contact).

Consequences
- Violations may result in removal of contributions or banning from the project after review.
